This dataset reports a vapor diffusion
crystallization experiment in which the
outcome is an ordinal measurement of the
crystallization called the crystal score

=========================================
                Summary
-----------------------------------------
    Number of Samples             918
    Dimensionality                 10
    Features:
        organic                  categorical (17 options)
        organic_molarity         continuous
        solvent                  categorical (3 options)
        solvent_molarity         continuous
        inorganic_molarity       continuous
        acid_molarity            continuous
        alpha_vial_volume        continuous
        beta_vial_volume         continuous
        reaction_time            discrete
        reaction_temperature     discrete
    Targets:
        crystal_score            ordinal (max)
